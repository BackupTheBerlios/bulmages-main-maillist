This is made by pluginbc_registroiva

In spain we have the obligation to declare all IVA in a separate document
(le 347) that means iva supported and iva repercuted. And we have the
obligatión to declare all clients and suppliers with amount of negoce that
grows 3000 euros.

When whe make an asiento with an account consideré IVA the
pluginbc_registroiva looks for the account of the supplier or client (in
group 673) where we impute that IVA. The message that you look sais you have
put IVA in an assiento where no client or supplier is involved and can't
register the 347.

Regard that we only use IVA accounts in register of facturas where a client
or supplier is ever involved.

If you don't want this remove the libpluginbc_registroiva. And this feature
will dissapear.
