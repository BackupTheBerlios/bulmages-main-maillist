What means "doesn't create anything"?

Check PostgreSQL permissions:

1) su root
2) su postgres
3) mcedit /var/lib/pgsql/data/pg_hba.conf

4) Change:
local   all         all                               ident
To:
local   all         all                               trust

5) Reset PostgreSQL daemon.
